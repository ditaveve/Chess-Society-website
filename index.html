About

A Society Built Around Chess

Founded to create a welcoming environment for players of all abilities, Warwick Chess Society hosts weekly casual games, competitive tournaments, lectures, and inter-university fixtures.

Whether you're learning how the knight moves or preparing for your next rated tournament, you'll find a strong and supportive community.
